About Us

You've come to the right place

Byford Veterinary Hospital is an independent, well equipped vet clinic in the centre of Byford.

Our clinic is homely and our facilities are modern. They include an operating theatre complete with digital x-ray, blood testing, anaesthetic monitoring, ultrasound and dental equipment; species specific consulting rooms and ample parking, along with experienced and caring team members (some of the best in Perth!).

More than that, we know how much your pets mean to you because we're animal lovers too. We promise to do our best for them and for you, with our compassionate care, hugs and cuddles and expert veterinary advice.
