About Our Clinic

Independent, homely, and passionately local — we're proud to be Byford's trusted small animal vet.

Who we are

A big smoochy fuss of your pets while they're here

Byford Vet Hospital is an independent, well equipped vet clinic in the centre of Byford. Our clinic is homely, our facilities modern, and our staff a delight.

The clinic team is passionate about the community and their animals. We make it our mission to give your pets the best possible care — and yes, that includes a big smoochy fuss while they're here!

Our modern facilities include an operating theatre complete with digital x-ray, blood testing, ultrasound, dental equipment and anaesthetic monitoring — along with species-specific consulting rooms and ample parking. With a combined team experience of over 40 years, we've got your pet covered.

We're located in central Byford on South West Highway, opposite Aldi and Lemas restaurant. No branch locations — just one dedicated, locally-owned clinic.
