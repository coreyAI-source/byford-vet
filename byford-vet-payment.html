Flexible Payment Options

When our animals get sick, we don't always have the money ready. We offer flexible payment plans so you can focus on your pet's care, not the bill.

Pay your way

VetPay & ZipPay — we've got you covered

To offer our clients more flexibility, we recommend VetPay or ZipPay in situations where upfront costs are a concern. Start your pet's treatment straight away and pay over time.

Payment Option 1

VetPay

VetPay is an independent Veterinary Treatment Finance service, designed to help pet owners pay for large or unexpected vet bills without the worry of high upfront costs.

VetPay settles the account with your vet directly, then sets up a payment plan for you, debiting your bank account or credit card with regular instalments.

1

Apply for VetPay finance online or in-clinic

2

Your pet receives treatment straight away

3

VetPay settles the bill and sets up your payment plan

Visit VetPay Website

Payment Option 2

ZipPay

ZipPay is a digital credit service that makes amounts up to $1,000 (ZipPay) or $3,000 (ZipMoney) available to spend at approved service providers like us.

Zip has almost 1.5 million customers across Australia and New Zealand and 18,000 retail partners. An independent company, backed by Westpac and NAB.

1

Sign up for a Zip account online — fast approval

2

Use your Zip credit at our clinic for treatment

3

Pay back over time with flexible repayments
