Meet Our Team

We have an amazing vet and nursing team to meet all of your small animal needs — and a vet or two who love exotics too.

Our Veterinarians

Experienced, caring, and passionate

You may request the same veterinarian at each appointment for continuity of care. Our combined team experience exceeds 40 years.

Dr Christine Stacy

Veterinarian

With over 28 years of experience in small animal medicine and surgery, Dr Christine has dedicated her career to the health and well-being of your beloved pets. Her extensive work at the Perth Cat Hospital has provided her with great knowledge of feline medicine.

While surgery is a passion of Dr Christine's, she equally cherishes the time spent interacting with her patients and educating their owners about their pets' health.

Surgery Feline Medicine Client Education Yoga Bush Walking

Dr Chris Cooper

Veterinarian

Dr Chris has been a steadfast member of the Byford team each Wednesday. He holds degrees in Pharmacology, Physiology, Animal Sciences, and Veterinary Medicine and Surgery, and has worked in over 13 countries across multiple diverse aspects of animal sciences.

Owner of Ajax (Mastiff Cross) and Malua (Feline Junk Yard Cat).

International Experience Pharmacology Diving Photography Electric Vehicles

Dr Georgia Taylor

Veterinarian

Dr Georgia graduated from Murdoch University in 2020 and spent the first few years of her career up north working in the sunshine. After relocating back to Perth, she joined Byford in April 2023 and has thoroughly enjoyed being back in the city!

She holds degrees in both Animal Science and Veterinary Medicine, and has a keen interest in small animal dentistry, animal welfare, and exotic wildlife medicine. Owner of senior pup Buddy.

Dentistry Wildlife Medicine Exotic Animals Scuba Diving
