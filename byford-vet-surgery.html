On-site surgical care

General surgical services right here at Byford

We provide general surgical services right here at the Byford Vet Hospital, which has an operating theatre equipped with everything required for the care of small animals.

Some of the more common procedures we can do:

  • Desexing (dogs, cats, rabbits, ferrets, guinea pigs and rats)
  • Caesareans
  • Soft tissue surgery and lump removals
  • Abdominal surgery
  • Minor orthopaedic procedures
  • Dental procedures (scale/polish and tooth extractions)
